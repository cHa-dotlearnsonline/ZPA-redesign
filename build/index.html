Vision

  • Achieve excellence in the provision of paediatrics and child health services

Mission

  • Achieving Excellence in the provision of quality health care to all children.
  • Foster quality medical education of health care professionals.
  • Promote research in all aspects of paediatrics and child care

Principles

  • Advancement of the field of paediatrics and child health
  • Providing advocacy for child health issues
  • Provision of leadership to decision makers in all spheres of society regarding paediatrics and child health
